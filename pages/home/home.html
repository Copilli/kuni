Are you sure you know about your emotions?

Have you ever feel angry? I think that your answer is "yes" 'cause all of us know about what is emotions, but we don't know how to manage them and what it means.

How it works:

This app is conformed to 4 pages, each one with one function. At home, we show you the information about the functions of the app and some additional information. At AboutUs we show the information about the team and additional facts about the apps.

What problem are we solving?

We saw that teenagers now are using the internet to find a solution to their problems, but sometimes they do not find the right way and in the end,it ends with a bigger problem. Sometimes that problem grows too much, and the teenagers don’t resist more, and they find the worst solution. You already know what it is.

How does our app solve the problem?

In our app there is information about the emotions that are bigger than the other emotions, and in there you can find a video and some advice to manage it in the correct way, not with the worst options. Our objective is to minimize the number of suicides in teenagers around 13-17 years, or if we can, in adults and kids too.

Why is this app the best way to solve the problem?

This is because the internet in actuality has a lot of impact on teenagers and on the people, and in that way, people receive information, but sometimes that information is wrong and it doesn't help with your problem. Now we’re doing that to give correct information to those people, and it could help them to manage their problems and emotions. .

Are there any similar solutions that already exist?

Yes, I think that there already exist some solutions of other companies, schools, or people. I saw some apps like this one that help with emotional problems too, but obviously we don’t copy that app, we designed our own app, with our own ideas, and in that way, “Kuni” appeared in our mind…

How will ensure that our technology minimizes the negative and maximizes the positive impact?

We’re not sure if it will have any impact on society, but we hope so. If it have an impact in our reality, I think that will help, and we hope that it is going to minimize the number of suicides in the teenagers, and it will help they to find how to manage their emotions.
