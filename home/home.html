KUNI: Manage Your Emotions

Welcome to our app! Here, you can express your feelings and learn to manage them in a safe, empowering way.

Do you want to know us?

Our team "Copilli's Girls" is made up of two secondary school students working to make emotional intelligence accessible to all.

Do you understand your emotions?

All of us experience emotions, but not everyone knows how to manage them. Our wiki helps you learn more about what you feel.

Explore the Wiki

How It Works

The app is structured around four key pages: a Home page to introduce the app, an Emotion Selector to choose how you feel, a Wiki to understand those emotions, and an About Us section to learn about our team and share feedback.

What Problem Are We Solving?

Teenagers today often turn to the internet for help with emotional challenges, but many sources are untrustworthy or even harmful. This misinformation can lead to worse outcomes — even tragedy. Our app aims to change that.

How Do We Solve It?

We provide verified emotional resources, videos, and practical advice tailored to the most intense emotions. Our mission is to reduce youth suicide by helping users manage their emotions in healthy ways.

Why Kuni?

The internet is already influential in young people’s lives. Instead of letting it spread misinformation, we’re using it to spread empathy, education, and hope.

Are There Other Solutions?

Yes, but we created Kuni from scratch with our own ideas and cultural perspective. The name "Kuni" means emotions in Mixtec — a tribute to our roots and our focus on emotional understanding.

How Will Kuni Make a Positive Impact?

We’re still testing and growing, but our vision is clear: to give young people the tools they need to face emotional challenges — with dignity, support, and knowledge.
